GPS Alarm Clock — Privacy Policy

Last updated: March 2026

Data We Collect

GPS Alarm Clock does not collect, transmit, or share any personal data. All information you enter — alarm times, labels, and geofence locations — is stored exclusively on your device in a local database. It never leaves your device.

Location Access

The app requests access to your device location (including background location) solely to check whether you are within a geofence boundary you defined. This check happens on-device. Your location coordinates are never sent to any server or third party.

Third Parties

This app has no analytics, no advertising SDKs, no crash reporting services, and no network calls other than loading map tiles from OpenStreetMap (a public, privacy-respecting tile service).

Data Deletion

Uninstalling the app deletes all data. There is nothing stored elsewhere to delete.

Contact

Questions? Open an issue on the project repository or contact the developer directly.
